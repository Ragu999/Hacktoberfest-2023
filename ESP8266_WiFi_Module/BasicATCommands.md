#List of AT Commands
##Basic AT Command: 
 ```AT\r\n```
##List Available networks:
```AT+CWLAP\r\n```
##Connect to a network: 
```AT+CWJAP=\"ssid\",\"password\"\r\n```
(ssid: netwok name, passwod: network password)
##Ping Google to check internet availability: 
```AT+PING=\"www.google.com\"\r\n```
